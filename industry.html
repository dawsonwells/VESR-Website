About the Industry

The art entertainment industry focuses on creating, displaying, and distributing artistic experiences, which typically involve interactive technology.

Business Role - Creative Animation Director

As a Creative Animation Director in this industry, you work on the conceptualization/visualization of digital art experiences. You also procure and manage digital assets, lead a team of artists and animators, and collaborate with technical teams (engineers, programmers, and architects) as you oversee the integration of art into the digital world.

Ethical Issue

A director must me mindful about the intellectual property of projects. Directors must ensure all artists are creating their own work (no pliagarism), as well as ensure that people don't misuse the assets created for the production.

Cybersecurity Issues + Best Practices

Issue #1 - Intellectual Property Theft: People inside and outside the company can steal digital assets for themselves, either to make money or to undermine company processes.

Best Practice: Make sure the team's work is protected by copright, so that you can legally claim ownership for all original work.

Issue #2 - Malware/Phishing: Malware can spread through phishing emails sent to employees. These emails try to get people to click on hyperlinks that download viruses or give access to the wrong party.

Best Practice: Always check the email sender. If it seems suspicious, report it and don't click on any links. Also, team members can use encrypted emails so that only people with the key can access them.
